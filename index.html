Our Story

Bandara Salon was founded in 2005 with a simple vision: to create a sanctuary where beauty, wellness, and artistry come together.

What began as a small neighborhood salon has grown into Colombo's premier beauty destination, known for our exceptional service, talented team, and commitment to using only the finest products.

Eco-Conscious

We prioritize sustainable practices and cruelty-free products.

Continuous Education

Our team regularly trains in the latest techniques and trends.

Community Focused

We support local charities and women's empowerment initiatives.

Our Philosophy

We believe beauty should enhance your natural features, not mask them. Our approach combines technical expertise with personalized care to help you look and feel your best.
